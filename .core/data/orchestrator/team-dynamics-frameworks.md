# Multi-Agent Team Dynamics Frameworks for Physics Research

## Core Philosophy: "팀의 힘은 개별 구성원의 합보다 크다" (The power of a team is greater than the sum of its individual members)

This comprehensive framework provides systematic approaches to understanding, managing, and optimizing team dynamics within the six-agent physics research system, integrating Korean collaborative principles with modern team science methodologies.
